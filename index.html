How to Play Mine Field?

Your goal is to find all the mines without touching them. Click or tap on a square to uncover it. Look at the numbers nearby as they tell you how many mines are close. If you think a square has a mine, right-click or hold to put a flag there. But be careful not to click on a mine by mistake.

The game has different levels with various grid sizes and mine amounts. You need to think fast and make smart moves to win. Each game is different, so it's always a new challenge. You can see how you compare to other players on the leaderboards and play online whenever you want.

If you're feeling cautious, you can choose to collect your winnings after each successful step by clicking the "Collect" button. This way, you secure what you've earned so far.
